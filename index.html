Activities to do in Rwanda

Rwanda, known as the "Land of a Thousand Hills," offers a variety of activities for visitors to enjoy. Here are some popular activities to do in Rwanda.

There are many activities

Have Fun and Enjoy Rwanda - from gorilla treks to lakeside sunsets, every moment is unforgettable.

Why Visit Rwanda?

One of Africa's safest and cleanest countries. Pristine national parks, world-class gorilla trekking, vibrant cities, and warm hospitality await you.

Kigali City Tour

Explore Africa's cleanest city — vibrant markets, the Genocide Memorial, and world-class coffee.

Gorilla Trecking

Experience the thrill of trekking through lush forests to see endangered mountain gorillas in their natural habitat.

Nyungwe Canopy Walk

Walk above the treetops on a canopy walkway in Nyungwe Forest, home to diverse wildlife and stunning views.

Lake Kivu Sailing

Enjoy sailing on the serene waters of Lake Kivu, surrounded by picturesque landscapes and charming lakeside towns.
